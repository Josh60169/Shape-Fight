NOTE: PLEASE USE A MOUSE FOR BEST EXPERIENCE


Hello Player!

In this game, you control a triangular ship that moves around and shoots enemy shapes.

Your objective is to last as long as you can by killing as many enemy shapes as you can without getting

shot 3 times.


- Use your mouse to point the ship in a given direction

- Use W to move foward

- Left click to shoot

- Space bar to move the shield to where ship is looking

(teleports or kills enemies on contact)


Good luck out there! Over and out.

SHAPE FIGHT

Hello Player!

In this game, you control a triangular ship that moves around and shoots enemy shapes.

Your objective is to last as long as you can by killing as many enemy shapes as you can without getting shot 3 times.


- Use your mouse to point the ship in a given direction

- Use W to move foward

- Left click to shoot

- Space bar to move the shield


Good luck out there! Over and out.
